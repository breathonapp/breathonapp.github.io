Breathon Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Breathon is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal anonymous data to help us improve the app experience.

DATA STORED LOCALLY

• Daily intentions and feelings
• Breathing session history
• App preferences (language, sound)
• Photos you choose to add

ANALYTICS

We use TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous usage signals (such as which features are used and app performance). TelemetryDeck does not collect any personally identifiable information, does not use advertising identifiers, and cannot identify individual users. All signals are anonymized and encrypted before transmission.

Learn more: telemetrydeck.com/privacy

IN-APP PURCHASES

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat does not store payment information. Learn more: revenuecat.com/privacy
